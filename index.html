What We Offer

Our Core Services

From expert analysis to staffing solutions, ETC Hearing Solutions Inc. supports every aspect of your occupational hearing conservation program.

Audiometric Analysis

In-depth review and analysis of audiometric test results by an experienced audiologist. Identifying shifts, threshold changes, and OSHA standard threshold shifts (STS).

Reporting

Comprehensive, clearly written reports for employers and employees. Compliance documentation prepared to meet OSHA and industry requirements.

Audiometric Testing

Hearing testing services using calibrated equipment. Onsite and mobile testing options available to minimize disruption to your workforce.

Staffing

Experienced occupational health personnel available for your hearing conservation program. CAOHC-certified technicians and qualified audiologists.

Workers' Comp Consultation

Expertise in Workers' Compensation regulations for noise-induced hearing loss across multiple states. Consultation available for claims and compliance.

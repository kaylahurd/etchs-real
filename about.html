About ETC Hearing Solutions

Our Story

Occupational Hearing Health Since 1970

ETC Hearing Solutions Inc. — known as ETCH's — is a privately owned occupational health company that has been serving employers and workers across the nation for over 55 years.

Founded in 1970, the company built its reputation on accurate audiometric analysis, professional reporting, and a thorough understanding of the regulations that govern occupational hearing conservation programs.

ETCH's specializes in the analysis and reporting side of hearing health — reviewing test results, identifying threshold shifts, preparing compliance documentation, and providing expert consultation on Workers' Compensation matters related to noise-induced hearing loss.

In addition to analysis and reporting, ETCH's offers audiometric testing services and qualified occupational health staffing to support employers at every level of their hearing conservation program.

Workers' Compensation Expertise

ETCH's maintains up-to-date knowledge of Workers' Compensation rules for noise-induced hearing loss across multiple states, providing consultation and documentation support for employers navigating claims.
